# Xperience-10M-Sample

This is a sample episode for [Xperience-10M](https://huggingface.co/datasets/ropedia-ai/xperience-10m).

One may download our videos and annotations and use [HOMIE-toolkit](https://github.com/Ropedia/HOMIE-toolkit) to understand our dataset.

Or you can download the rrd file and use rerun (0.29.0) to visualize the whole 3D/4D structured annotations.
